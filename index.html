Binance Web3 Extension — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Binance Web3 Extension (the “Extension”, “we”, “us”) handles information when you use it. The Extension is a non-custodial crypto wallet: your seed phrase, private keys, and passwords are stored locally in encrypted form on your device and are never transmitted to us.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We never collect, transmit, or store your seed phrase, private keys, or wallet password.
  • We have no access to your funds and cannot move them.
  • We do not sell your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

  • Usage & analytics data (only if you consent): anonymous product-interaction events, feature usage, and device/browser information, processed via PostHog, Segment, and Amplitude. You can disable this at any time in the Extension’s settings.
  • Crash & error diagnostics: technical error reports processed via Sentry to help us fix bugs.
  • Public wallet addresses: used to fetch balances, transaction history, and token data from public blockchains and third-party data providers.
  • Email address: only if you voluntarily opt in to our newsletter. You can unsubscribe at any time.

3. How We Use Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Extension.
  • To diagnose and fix technical problems.
  • To understand which features are used (in aggregate) when you opt in.
  • To send newsletter communications you have requested.

4. Third-Party Services

The Extension interacts with public blockchain networks (Avalanche, Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana and others) and third-party data and infrastructure providers to display balances and broadcast transactions. These services operate under their own privacy policies.

5. Data Storage & Security

All sensitive wallet data is encrypted and stored locally on your device. We use industry-standard measures to protect any data processed by our analytics and diagnostics providers.

6. Your Choices

  • Disable analytics at any time in Settings.
  • Unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time.
  • Uninstall the Extension to remove all locally stored data.

7. Children’s Privacy

The Extension is not intended for anyone under the age of 18.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above reflects the most recent revision.
